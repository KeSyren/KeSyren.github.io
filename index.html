About Me

Qing Wang is a Ph.D. studying in School of Computing and Information Sciences at Florida International University under the supervision of Prof. Tao Li, Prof. S. S. Iyengar and Prof. Shu-Ching Chen. Qing entered the Ph.D. program in Fall 2014, immediately after obtaining her Master and Bachelor degree in Computer Science from Xidian University in 2013 and Zhengzhou University in 2009. Her research focuses on interactive recommender system [irs_ppt], multi-armed bandit algorithm [intro_ppt], and event mining. My work in event mining can be found in ACM KDD 2017 tutorial System Event Mining: Algorithms and Applications. Her dissertation is "Intelligent Data Mining Techniques for Automatic Service Management". [ppt]
